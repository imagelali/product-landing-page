electric bicycles

After decades of development, electric bicycles have become hugely popular since 2000, with a more serious future ahead of them. For sports, as a status symbol and as a daily means of transport. Today, it is only natural for a bank manager not to commute 4-10 km a day by car, as you can travel faster and healthier with an electric bike.

Varieties:

Pedelec

The electric motor uses a sensor to assist the cyclist depending on the power of the pedaling.

S-pedelec

In addition to the pedal torque, there is also a throttle lever on the steering wheel to control the assist.

E-bike

The electric assist is controlled without pedaling, only with the throttle lever on the steering wheel.
